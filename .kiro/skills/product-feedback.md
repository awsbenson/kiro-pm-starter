# Product Feedback Intelligence

## When to Use
- Multiple escalations show the same pattern
- Customer requests a feature that doesn't exist
- Preparing input for product roadmap planning
- Writing a product gap analysis

## Feedback Report Structure
1. **Pattern Summary** — What's recurring across escalations
2. **Customer Impact** — Which customers, what tier, revenue at risk
3. **Frequency** — How often, trending up or down
4. **Current Workaround** — What customers do today
5. **Competitive Gap** — Do competitors solve this?
6. **Recommendation** — Build, buy, partner, or accept risk
7. **Priority Signal** — P0 (blocking renewals) to P3 (nice-to-have)

## Rules
- Always quantify with data (N escalations, $X revenue at risk)
- Include customer quotes when available
- Separate "what customers ask for" from "what they actually need"
- Link to specific escalation IDs for traceability
