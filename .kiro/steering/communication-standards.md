# Communication Standards

## Audience-Specific Rules

### Executive (CISO, VP)
- Bullet format, no jargon
- Lead with business impact
- Include "risk and ask" section
- Max 10 bullets

### Engineering / R&D
- Reproduction steps, environment details
- Severity justification with evidence
- What logs/data are available vs needed
- Clear requirements, not vague asks

### Customer
- Acknowledge impact first
- Then status, then next steps
- Calm, ownership-oriented tone
- No internal blame or finger-pointing

### Sales / Account Team
- Renewal risk assessment
- Customer sentiment (1-5 scale)
- Talking points and what NOT to promise
- Timeline expectations

### Product Manager
- Feature gap description
- Customer segment impact
- Revenue at risk
- Roadmap implication
